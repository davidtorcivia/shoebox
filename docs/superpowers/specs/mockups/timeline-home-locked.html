Player notes

  • Its own room: the decade palette collapses to its deep end (iron black → shrimp brown → an ember of benihi) so footage glows, but it's still unmistakably the '90s world — every decade's player room derives from its palette.
  • Controls as typography: monospace timecode, hairline track with a dawn-colored elapsed line, plain-text VOL / 1× / FULL. No buttons, no pills, no icons ripped from a UI kit. Controls fade out during playback, return on move/tap.
  • Colorist-friendly keys: space play/pause, J/K/L shuttle, ←/→ frame-step, ↑/↓ prev/next item, F fullscreen, M mute.
  • The right rail is the memory: date with tape provenance, description, people with ages computed for that day, tags, albums, comments ("Add a memory…"). On mobile it stacks under the video.
  • Photos open in this same room minus the control row.
